FRUITS

A fruit is the part of a flowering plant that contains the seeds. The skin of a fruit may be thin, tough, or hard. Its insides are often sweet and juicy. But some fruits, including nuts are dry.

MANGO

•Tropical usually large ovoid or oblong fruit with a firm yellowish-red skin, hard central stone, and juicy aromatic pulp.The fruit varies greatly in size and character. Its form is oval, round, heart-shaped, kidney-shaped, or long and slender.

ORANGE

•Oranges are round, juicy citrus fruits with a bright orange rind and segmented, sweet, and tangy flesh, known for their high vitamin C content and are a popular food and juice source.
